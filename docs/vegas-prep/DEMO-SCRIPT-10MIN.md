## Act 3: The Partnership Model (2 minutes)

### How Referrals Work

> "Here's how the partnership works in practice:"

**[Count on fingers or show partnership flyer]**

1. **Family tells you they're interested in sea burial** — or you mention it as an option during arrangement
2. **You hand them our co-branded brochure** or direct them to waterandashburials.org
3. **They book through our site** — your referral is tracked automatically via UTM parameters
4. **We handle everything** — from scheduling to ceremony to EPA compliance
5. **You earn a referral fee** — deposited quarterly, no invoicing required from your end
6. **Family comes back to you** thanking you for the incredible recommendation

### Co-Marketing Support

> "We don't just take referrals and disappear. We provide:"

- **Co-branded marketing materials** — flyers, brochures with your logo
- **Digital assets** — images and copy for your website or social media
- **Training session** — 30-minute overview for your arrangement staff
- **QR codes** — place in your facility, links to your personalized landing page
- **Joint testimonials** — happy families mention both of us

### The Numbers

> "The average sea burial with us is $2,000. Even with a modest referral fee, that's meaningful revenue — and it's revenue you're currently leaving on the table."

> "Most funeral homes we work with see 2-5 sea burial referrals per month once they start mentioning it. Some in coastal areas see double that."
