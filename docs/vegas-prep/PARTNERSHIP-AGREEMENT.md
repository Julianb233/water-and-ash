## 3. Referral Process

### How Referrals Work

1. Partner identifies a family interested in sea burial services
2. Partner provides family with Water & Ash Burials information (brochure, website link, or direct introduction)
3. Family contacts Water & Ash Burials via website, phone (619-928-9160), or email (info@waterandashburials.org)
4. Referral is tracked via partner-specific UTM link or verbal mention at intake
5. Water & Ash Burials handles all scheduling, coordination, ceremony, and compliance
6. Referral fee is credited upon completed service

### Partner-Specific Tracking

Each partner receives:
- A unique referral URL (e.g., `waterandashburials.org/funeral-homes?ref=PARTNER_CODE`)
- A QR code linking to the partner-specific URL
- Co-branded marketing materials with tracking built in
