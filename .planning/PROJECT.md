# Water & Ash Burials

## What This Is

A premium website and CRM integration for Water & Ash Burials, a San Diego-based sea burial service. The platform replaces an amateur Squarespace site with a professional, luxury-positioned web presence that captures leads, handles bookings with deposits, and integrates with GoHighLevel CRM for pipeline management and automations.

## Core Value

Convert website visitors into booked sea burial ceremonies by presenting Water & Ash as the premium, heart-centered alternative to traditional cemetery burials - a celebration of life on the ocean.
